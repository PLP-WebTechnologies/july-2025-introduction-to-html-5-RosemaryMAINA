About Us

Green Earth is a non-profit organization dedicated to environmental conservation and education. We empower communities to make sustainable choices for the health of our planet.

Our Mission

To raise awareness about climate change, promote sustainable practices, and protect natural resources for future generations.

Our Projects

Tree Planting Campaign

We organize annual tree planting events to restore deforested areas and improve air quality.

Plastic-Free Awareness

Our plastic-free campaign educates people on reducing single-use plastic in everyday life.
